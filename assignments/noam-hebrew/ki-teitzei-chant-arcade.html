Best 10-Minute Routine

  1. Listen while tracking: Play the model audio or contour demo once while the student points to each word.
  2. Echo one word: Tap the hardest word and repeat it three times.
  3. Echo two-word chunks: Combine adjacent words before attempting the whole line.
  4. Record and compare: The student listens back and names one thing that improved.
  5. End with a win: Play one short game or mark one line practiced.

What to Listen For

Hebrew accuracy: Are the letters and vowels being read correctly?
Trope movement: Does the voice move in the right direction and pause in the right place?
Confidence: Can the student restart without getting stuck?

Important Audio Note

The app’s built-in audio uses contour tones to make trope patterns visible and hearable. It does not certify nusach accuracy. For final preparation, compare against a real model recording from the tutor, cantor, rabbi, or family-approved source.
